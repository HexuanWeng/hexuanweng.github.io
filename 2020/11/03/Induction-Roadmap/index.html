Melbourn Cup! Welcome on Board!

Posted on 2020-11-03 Edited on 2021-08-28 In Simple Life
Welcome on board! Hannah!

Day One Log

  • get your badge from Michelle of Reception
  • Meet and Greet with Nathan / Justin
  • Meet your assigned onboarding
  • Take a tour of the office. Find the toilets, pantry/ lunch room
  • complete the online training from ASI Solution

Today is my first day at ASI Solution. I was too nervous to fall asleep yesterday. I woke up at 7 am which is much earlier than I used to. I arrived at the company around 8:30, waiting for Huy at the cafe. My first day in ASI Solution is also a day of Melbourn Cup. The company has a big BBQ gathering outside of our company.
I didn’t know that I am the only employee who has actually no background on Microsoft platform but work as a Microsoft Surface Specialist & Power Apps Developer. Justin prefers to hire someone who has 8 years working experience but Huy still decided to hire me because he said he can see the huge potential I possessed. I really appreciate this opportubity and I hope that I can contribute to the company as well.
Huy led me to meet everyone in the company. “Welcome on board” is the most frequent statement I heared today. This is such a wonderful experience to work with folks from different backgrounds. Most of them are Aussies and sometimes I could feel the language barriers.
Today, I feel sleepy because I could not fall asleep last night until 3 am. I met some intersting and friendly colleagues today, like Alex, Oscar, Andrew~ Hope that I can remember all the names of our company within one month~
Tomorrow is another training day. Looking forward to it!

Day Two Log

The second day at ASI Solutions. I feel fulfilled actually. I spent the whole day in learning Power Apps and keeping updated with Justin about the Fulcrum App. This is such a different feeling I have after I join the team. It is quiet laid back and I love our team with wonderful team members. I also feel excited that I will participate in many exams as well as getting Microsoft certificates.
Huy treated me an Aussie Burger at lunch to welcome me to join the team. And I also applied for the yoga and bootcamp courses via Michelle. I don’t have any feelings of tireness but I feel happy and positive. I gradually form some good habits after I join the team, for example, I start having breakfast on a daily basis, I try to do more exercises with colleagues four days a week, and I also form a habit of drinking lots of warm water when working at the office.
I hope that I can become an asset to the company I work for and I hope that I can fit in earlier~
BTW, today is also an important day worldwide because we will see the result of US Election 2020: Donald Trump vs Joe Biden. The election is still in progress. Let’s see the results tomorrow~

Week One Log

It’s been one week since I started at ASI Solution. I feel that I have already fit in. I start to talk with staff from other teams and I will attend the Yoga Class and Bootcamp next week.
Talking to people from different backgrounds opened my mind, to be honest. Actually, I keep a close eye on US Election 2020. And the result come out and Joe Biden becomes US president. On Friday, when we met at the lunch room during our break, I ask Oscar about his opinion on US president election. His reply really astonished me, because I have never thought it in that point of view. America is a country that I knew when I was in primary school, but I have never been there. I didn’t notice the problems of the health care system there. And they support Biden for some reason and there are some certain reasons they also want to support Trump. Actually, many people do not like both of them, but they have to pick one.
I started a more regular life. This week, I also receive a new Microsoft Surface Laptop 3!! This new gift really made my day!
